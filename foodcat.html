Margherita Pizza: Traditional Italian pizza

Margherita pizza is a classic Italian dish that is known for its simplicity and delicious flavors. It is named after Queen Margherita of Savoy, who visited Naples in 1889 and was served a pizza that was made to resemble the Italian flag with its red tomato sauce, white mozzarella cheese, and green basil leaves. The Queen loved the pizza so much that it became an instant hit and is now one of the most popular pizza varieties around the world. continue reading....
