Nauvari: The Traditional Maharashtrian saree

Navvari or Nauvari is a traditional Maharashtrian saree that has its roots in the Maratha Empire. The name "navvari" literally means "nine-yard" and refers to the length of the saree. Unlike other sarees that are six yards long, the navvari saree is made of nine yards of fabric, which is draped around the body in a unique style. continue reading....
